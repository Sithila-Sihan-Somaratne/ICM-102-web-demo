Territory


Italy's flag.

Italy is a southern european country. It confines with France , Switzerland , Austria , Slovenia , San Marino and the Vatican City and it's washed by the Meditarrean Sea which has got some few smaller seas such as: the Adriatic sea, the Ionian sea, the Sicilian Sea, the Sardinian Sea and the Tyrrhenian sea. Its flag is green, white and red. Italy is constituted by 41.6% of hills, 35.2% of mountains and the 23.2% of plains. Its surface is about 302.073 km² and its population is around 59 milions. The highest mountain is the Mont Blanc which his altitude is about 4.807 m. The largest plain is the Po Valley where its surface is about 47.820 km². The longest river is the Po River which is long 652 km while the biggest lake is the Lake of Garda where its surface is about 370 km².
